Branding

Branding... the difference between
“old crap” and “vintage must have”

Here’s the cold hard truth. You can't please all of the people all of the time. Sorry if that seems defeatist - but it's true. And let's be honest, would you really want to? If brands marched to the beat of the same drum, this world would be one dimensional and boring as hell.

So, you gotta stop trying; stop making brand sacrifices to please the man. Know yourself and you'll get to know the people you're meant to market and engage with. Be true to your founding ethos and your brand will move forward with great integrity. While you may not be liked by some, you'll be respected by all.
